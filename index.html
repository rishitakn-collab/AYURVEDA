🌿 Herbs

Hing (Asafoetida)

A powerhouse spice that keeps your blood flowing smoothly while helping manage high blood pressure. It sparks the nervous system into action, easing convulsions, syncope, and hysteria. Plus, it’s a digestive champion—boosting appetite, releasing trapped gas, and soothing spasms naturally.

Moringa – The Miracle Tree

Packed with vitamins A, C, E, calcium, potassium, and protein, moringa is a nutritional powerhouse. Its antioxidants battle cellular damage, while anti-inflammatory compounds soothe arthritis and other aches. From speeding up wound healing to supporting blood sugar, liver, kidney, and even respiratory health, moringa is a natural multitasker for total wellness.

Basil – The Healing Leaf

Packed with antioxidants like eugenol and lutein, basil fights cellular damage and supports heart and immune health. Its anti-inflammatory and digestive-boosting properties ease aches and stomach troubles, while holy basil helps the body adapt to stress. Bonus: it naturally battles bacteria, keeping you healthy inside and out.

Cilantro – The Zesty Detoxer

Bursting with antioxidants and essential nutrients, cilantro shields your body from oxidative stress while supporting digestion. It helps manage blood sugar, fights harmful microbes, and even aids in detoxifying heavy metals. Bonus perk: its calming properties may help ease anxiety naturally.

Turmeric (Curcumin) – Golden Healer

Turmeric packs powerful antioxidant punch, neutralizing harmful free radicals while boosting your body’s defenses. Its anti-inflammatory magic soothes chronic inflammation, supports digestion, skin, and immunity, while also nurturing brain health.

Kalonji (Nigella sativa)

Supports digestion by reducing bloating, gas, and indigestion. Helps regulate blood sugar, improves cholesterol, boosts immunity, supports skin and hair health, and shows anticancer potential with thymoquinone.

About Project Anvay

Manual Ayurvedic diet charts are inefficient and lack scientific precision. Traditional methods often rely on generalized approaches that don't account for individual variations and modern nutritional understanding.

Our software automates patient-specific Ayurvedic diet charts by combining modern nutrition data with traditional principles, ensuring each recommendation is both scientifically sound and rooted in time-tested Ayurvedic wisdom.
